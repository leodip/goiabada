Goiabada was developed to be an easy-to-use solution for user management. Plenty of alternatives exist, but this is my personal take on the concept.

I'm committed to making Goiabada forever free and open source. I want you to self-host it and have total control over the code and data.

## The name

The name is inspired by a Brazilian sweet made with guava. I was keen on choosing a word that started with "Go".

![Goiabada - Brazilian guava sweet](/img/about1.png)
